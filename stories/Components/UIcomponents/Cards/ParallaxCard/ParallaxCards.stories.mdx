# Parallax Cards
The Parallax Cards component defines a card of information data with some items- Title, Paragraph and CTA Link. 

### Overview
Parallax is a trend where the background image or the content of the web page changes at different speeds while scrolling.  

#### When to use:
- To create a 3-D effect.

### Formatting

#### Default
The Parallax uses a Grid system to manage the different sizes. It is a multi-usage component that creates boxes that are usually teasing some kind of content.

### Behaviors

#### States

1. **Default:** The Image is placed in either part of the grid and the Title, Paragraph and CTA Link in the other part. As we scroll the page the image remains constant, while the contents move slightly up or down depending on the direction of the scrolling. The CTA link is expandable.
2. **Mobile:** The Title is placed at the top of the card, followed by Image, Paragraph and CTA Link. The images scroll horizontally with a scroll bar at the top. The CTA link is expandable.
